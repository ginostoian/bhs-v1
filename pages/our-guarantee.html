The Better Homes Studio Guarantee

Problems do sometimes happen and there is no way you can avoid them. That is why we offer to all of our clients a workmanship guarantee.

Depending on the type of project we work on, you will benefit of up to 2 years of guarantee.

This means that as long as the problem has been caused by our workmanship, we will come out and fix it for free.

Our guarantee does not cover item breakage, but we will be able to come and fix the issue for a discounted price.

Either way, for any claims on our warranty, we ask you to use the contact form on our website and let us know.

Once we receive your enquiry we will process it and get in touch with you as soon as possible to get the issue fixed.

1. Decorating Guarantee

Please keep in mind that our decorating services come with a 1 year guarantee. We take pride in our work, therefore we are happy to issue the following guarantee:

➔ We guarantee to return and correct any defects that may have occurred as a result of our workmanship within a 1 year period from the date the project was completed

The issue has to be caused by our workmanship. We do not guarantee the work of other companies or tradesmen.

All claims should be sent to us over email with a description of the fault or issue and photos in order for us to evaluate it.

Upon confirmation of the fault we require between 7 and 14 working days to book in a team to correct the issue, unless the issue is urgent.
