Modern bathrooms

Geometrical shapes, simple color palettes with sleek bold accents or tones of neutral colors.

Modern bathrooms are predominantly simplistic. Decorative bathroom tiles add a sort of unmatched charm to the entire space that cranks things up a notch.

If you really wish to put the X-Factor in extra, don‚Äôt for once shy away from bold colours encircling a statement bathtub or shower.

Industrial bathrooms

Exposed pipes, ducts, tubes, and wires are commonly found in industrial style bathrooms.

When considering an industrial style bathroom renovation you can go either strong or soft.

Whichever option you decide to go with, you can use metro tiles, grey colors, bare brick walls, exposed shelving and black shower frames.

Let's discuss your project

Traditional bathrooms

Traditional style bathrooms are classic and elegant in design.

Traditional bathroom designs typically take their cue from what is currently trending in the design world, with materials and decor that are respective of their historical periods.

Keep your accessories clean and simple, you could consider monogramming for your towels and bath mats.

Let's discuss your project

Victorian bathrooms

This design style often features a freestanding bath as the center piece.

Victorian bathrooms are characterized by the elegant design elements commonly found in the grand homes of the Victorian era.

In general, the design for Victorian bathrooms focuses on beauty, elegance, and comfort.

Do you offer any guarantees?

All our bathroom renovations come with a 2 year workmanship guarantee. Although we do not cover items breaking, any issues that arise from our workmanship, we will come and fix it, no charge! To find out more about our guarantee click here.

Do you do just the build phase?

Already know how you want your bathroom to look like? No problem, we do take on bathroom refurbishments only with no design.

Do you offer free quotes?

Yes. Our quotes are free of charge and do not carry any obligation on your part.

How long will my project take?

Bathroom renovations usually take between 10 and 14 days. That being said, every project is unique, and some might be quirky, needing a bit more time than this. To find out more please contact us by clicking here.

How are you going to quote for my project?

We can estimate the cost of your bathroom refurbishment before even visiting your property. If the estimation fits in your budget, we will set an on-site meeting and finalise the quote. To get a free, quick bathroom renovation estimate, click here.

How long will the toilet be out?

If you're living in the property while we are doing the job, we will install the toilet at the end of each day so you can use it. We will try to do the same for the bath.
