Frequent Questions

Can't find an answer? Get in touch!

Who is Better Homes Studio?

We are a construction, interior renovation and design company. We have been working in London for over 10 years and have established experience in the field.

What type of projects do you undertake?

Here is a list the projects we undertake, although it is not complete:

  • Interior Design
  • Bathroom renovation
  • Kitchen renovation
  • Full house refurbishment
  • House extensions
  • Loft conversions
  • Boiler installations
  • Electrical rewiring
  • And more..

Do you offer any guarantees?

All our home renovations come with a 1 year workmanship guarantee. Although we do not cover items breaking, any issues that arise from our workmanship, we will come and fix it, no charge! To find out more about our guarantee click here.

Do you do just the build phase?

Already know how you want your home to look like? No problem, we do take on house refurbishments envisioned by you.

Do you offer free quotes?

Yes. Our quotes are free of charge and do not carry any obligation on your part.

How long will my project take?

The duration of a home renovation varies greatly dependant on the scope of the work. If you're planning on painting, it can take a couple of day. A full refurbishment can take a couple of months. To find out more please contact us by clicking here.

How are you going to quote for my project?

We can estimate the cost of your home refurbishment before even visiting your property. If the estimation fits in your budget, we will set an on-site meeting and finalise the quote. To get a free, quick home refurbishment estimate, click here.

How long will I have to move out for?

You are not obliged to move out during the house refurbishment. As long as you are aware that your home will be a construction site and there will be dust and noise, we take no issue with you living in the house.

Do you offer refferal awards?

Yes. For every client reffered by you that we end up working with, you will win a prize. Please contact us to find out more about this program.
