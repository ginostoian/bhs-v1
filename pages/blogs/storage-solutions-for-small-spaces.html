15 Storage Solutions for every room in your

Many of us have the habit of collecting a lot of things. Useful or not, we like to collect various things in our homes in the hope that one day we might use them. This is fine up to a point until we realize that we have nowhere to store them, and our house becomes less and less roomy.

No matter how big or small the space we live in is, when it comes to storage, we all have difficulties in finding the most suitable and at the same time fashionable place where to put things because smart storage does not always mean that everything has to be hidden. With the right strategies, you can live small and store well. We believe that creativity is the answer to many of life‚Äôs problems, especially when we talk about a house. Organizing your home does not have to happen all at once. We will show you how to organize your home room by room with creative and practical storage ideas for bathrooms, kitchens, living areas, and more. Follow these room organization tips to establish order one room at a time.

Make every inch of your home count through well-thought-out and space-saving storage solutions.

Storage solutions for small spaces

If you are planning a partial redecoration or looking for effective storage solutions only for certain rooms that fit into an affordable budget, this article comes to your aid with the best and most ingenious storage ideas, no matter if you're looking for compact bathroom solutions or small living room ideas.

Most of the tips and tricks that will be presented in this article can be used in any room in the house

1. Cubbyholes

A very popular design these days is given by these cubbyholes which can be both incorporated into the wall since the construction of the space, but which can also be found in speciality stores like Ikea. They are used both to store things and to delimit spaces when you want to separate certain areas in a large room.

2. Make more out of an alcove

Alcoves are the perfect space to build shelves and storage units which will look wonderful in your rooms, without taking up much space and are a great storage idea for a contemporary scheme. Also, make the most of an awkward recess in the wall and with a simple but clever design for alcoves you can transform that space into something comfortable and practical like a narrow bookcase on its side to create an alcove bench for seating and storage. Put storage baskets under the bench, and on top of it, you can put some pillows and beds to create an ideal reading space.

‚ÄòOpen bookshelves can look great, with the books becoming part of the scheme,‚Äô says Tim Newbold of Domus

If you want to be even more practical, instead of a bench you can incorporate a desk, thus turning that area into a mini-office.

3. Hang Floating Shelves

Sometimes, in small spaces, wall cabinets can seem like they take up too much space, but if we still have a bit of extra wall space, a pair of floating shelves are an excellent idea. You can make the most of the potential of these shelves to store things by putting a few hooks under the bottom shelf for hanging towels, mugs, utensils and other daily essentials for easy access to your most-used items.

4. Use the back of the doors

One of the most clever small space organization idea is using the back of doors. Most of the time this idea is put into practice only with the bathroom doors where we hang towels, robes or clothes, but All doors provide valuable storage space in small rooms.

For example, in kitchens, you can use the back of the door to hang the cleaning products so that the little ones can't reach or you can hang a spice rack. Also, the back of the door in the room can be very useful for hanging a mirror, accessories or bags

5. Use the space around a door

As we said, when we live in a small space, creativity is the best solution to solve all our problems. The more we think outside the box, the more we will discover places to store that we didn't even think about until then. For example, the space around a door is a wasted space if we don't use it. By building some deep shelves you can create a super library above the door, or even decorate your room with a few lovely green plants or accessories.

6. Make the most of the window area

Any place that can have a few things on it is welcome in small spaces and this includes windowsills, too. If you have them, they‚Äôre a perfect spot for plants, or any other small objects you can think of. Also, the space under windows can be very valuable to put a closet to hide different things.

7. Fill the space under the stairs

In a compact home, every corner counts! Make the most of the space under the stairs by adding small storage solutions. If the space under the stairs is closed then we can put boxes and baskets with things that we don't use very often, but also a small closet where we can store our shoes.

If the space is open, then we can make bespoke cabinets to put everything from clothes to ceramics, or we can leave the space open and place a desk or a clothes hanger.

8. Opt for a media storage system

Use the TV to your advantage and turn it into a focal point to build storage around. This system can be composed of both modular parts and a compact part, everything depends on how your space allows. You can bespoke build or buy these storage units from Ikea or other furniture stores.

9. Use furniture to divide spaces

Due to the lack of space, many of us assign several purposes to a single room. Most of the time, the kitchen is in the same place as the living room, and sometimes even in the bedroom. For such situations, the smartest solution is to use a piece of furniture that separates the spaces and also creates a storage place.

Another option that offers the same effect is placing a storage unit behind the sofa or bed.

10. Store under the bed

Hiding things under the bed is one of the oldest storage methods used. The bed is the piece of furniture that takes up the most space in a room, therefore we must take advantage of this and exploit this occupied area

Some beds already have built-in drawers that keep your clothes or things stored away from dust, but if you have a simple bed without those built-in spaces, you can find many boxes with lids or individual drawers of different sizes and materials that can protect your things very well

11. Keep the hallway organized

A bench with storage underneath is an ideal way to keep hallway clutter under control. In addition to giving the place a comfortable design, such a piece of furniture can be a good place to store slippers, accessories for the cold season, bags or anything else that you currently have in the hallway and want to hide.

A spacious ottoman with a raised lid is brilliant, but also a simple bench under which you can put different baskets or storage boxes looks very good in the hallway. The wall behind this piece of furniture can be used to hang some hooks where you can hang your clothes, umbrellas or hats.

12. Hang Basket Shelves

This storage method is very cheap, but with a great stylistic effect on any of your rooms. They can be mounted very easily and placed in any corner of the room, for example, they are very useful in a bathroom where we want to have make-up, body lotions, accessories and even hand towels handy.

13. Use Pegboards

We love how pegboard it's a super problem solver with customisable storage add-ons and it's super easy to use in any room from your house. We can hang anything on a pegboard, and in the kitchen, it is a real help when it comes to finding storage solutions for pans and pots, but also for heavy utensils such as large spoons and ladles. All of these can be hung on the pegboard so that they no longer take up precious space in the closets.

We can also hang containers with snacks, but also all kinds of accessories that we want to have at hand.

These pegboards are also often used in the garage, where the utensils can be kept in order and visible.

14. Use Shelf Risers

A shelf riser for the interior of the cabinets is a wonderful idea to double the storage space. That way, we can make the most of all the space in a closet and not only that, we can also use them independently to better organize things on the kitchen counter, desk or work table.

15. Swing-Out Storage

Swing-out shelves are a very practical option for corner cabinets that are often underutilized, their positioning being inconvenient, but with the help of these folding shelves, we can get the most out of those spaces.

Finding these and utilizing them is the first step towards maximizing storage potential in smaller homes‚Äô, says professional organizer Brenda Scott of Tidy My Space

In addition to a rich imagination and incredible creativity that you have to prove when you live in a small space, another very important feature in creating space is flexibility. Regardless of how limited the storage options are in a room, cramming a lot of things into it is not a solution because that way we will make it non-functional.

Therefore, we have to resort to different tricks that involve creativity and a bit of flexibility when choosing things, meaning that they must be functional in several situations Also, there are some essential ways to maximize storage in a small space such as dual-purpose furniture with hidden storage, such as the ottoman style.

Thinking vertically is also a great way of claiming backspace. Vertical surfaces offer plenty of storage potential, from shelving and hanging organizers to racks and rails. Also, all storage methods that do not involve occupying the floor can be a suitable idea

Rule number one when it comes to organizing small storage is not to waste space with things we don't need and don't use. Schedule regular clearouts to ensure clutter doesn't build up again.

Tailoring storage to your exact organizational needs is another space-saver, but if the budget does not allow it, there are many cheap methods, presented above, which can provide you with storage space, even in the smallest places.

Think outside the "room" to organize your things with the most intelligent storage methods, and if you are thinking of renovating your house to better optimize the space, don't forget that we are at your disposal with the best and most practical design ideas.
