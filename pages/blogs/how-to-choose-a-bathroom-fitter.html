Tips on what to look after when hiring a bathroom contractor

It might not seem like it but the bathroom is one of the most important rooms in your home. Think about it. It's the place where you get ready, where you clean yourself and where you "meditate"..cough!

Because of its importance to the whole family, the bathroom should not only look pretty but also be functional.

Did you know that a refurbished bathroom increases the sell value of a house?

For the above reasons, some people are renovating their bathrooms every 5 years or so. Others only renovate when their budgets allow them to do so.

Either way, renovating a bathroom is not an easy task. It is a costly endeavour that will require your attention if you wish to get the best results.

Choosing the right contractor for your bathroom refurbishment project is crucial as they will be the people in charge of making your dream bathroom come true. The more experience they have, the fewer problems will occur during the project and this will make the process much easier for you.

Below we will list some things you should keep in mind when you are hiring a company to manage your bathroom renovation project.

1. Check the contractors' experience

In building, experience is very important and is what sets contractors apart. A company or a sole trader that has completed 50 bathroom renovations will be much better at spotting potential problems with the project in advance, than someone who has worked and completed 3 projects. The more experienced the contractor the more advice he can offer you when making important decisions like e.g. 'what type of paint to use', 'problems with layout changes', 'water pressure problems that may arise when changing bathroom fittings'.

The best way to check a contractor's experience is by looking over their online presence. They will usually have different profiles and accounts with reviews that showcase the projects that they worked on. If the contractor worked with someone you know, even better, ask that person about the experience they've had with them.

2. Read the quote thoroughly

The company that you are planning to hire should always send you a written proposal usually called a quote. This should have their details, the date the quote has been written, the services that are being quoted and their respective prices and what else is included in the price.

It is very important to read the quote carefully and understand exactly what you are paying for as this is usually where you could end up getting burned.

The quotes provided by Better Homes Studio state exactly the materials that are included in the price, the materials that the client is expected to purchase, the price for each service and the total of the services plus the VAT. We also have a disclaimer for the client that any service required that is not present in the quote will have to be priced separately.

3. The quote should not cost

There are some situations where companies go the extra mile when quoting and offer the client 3D renders and whatnot and that quote will cost you upfront, which I think is fair.

You should raise an eyebrow when a company or sole trader tries to charge you for a simple, normal

At Better Homes Studio we try and make the quoting process as easy as possible for the client. With this in mind we offer a couple of options to get your bathroom refurbishment quote: - on-site visit with an estimator - zoom or facetime call with an estimator - easy and quick online bathroom quotation form

4. Check their availability

Sometimes companies are booked for months even though they are quoting for new projects. Always discuss when you would like to start your bathroom renovation to check if they have teams available for that period.

Connected to this is that you should always ask your contractor to estimate the time it will take them to complete the project. The answer to this question will let you know if the contractor knows what he's doing or not.

A bathroom renovation usually takes between 10 and 16 days to complete depending on the difficulty. If you have someone telling you that they will complete your bathroom in 3 days, that is a massive red flag.

5. Always ask your contractor if they offer any guarantee

Guarantees are very important especially when talking about bathroom refurbishments.

A lot of the time things do go wrong especially over time. A leak can appear at some point, grouting can wear off, silicone might need to be reapplied etc. If these problems appear due to the workmanship of the contractor he should be liable to come and fix them free of charge for some time after the project has been completed.

Better Homes Studio offers a 2-year Bathroom Fitting guarantee to every client. This means that any problem caused by our workmanship occurs in this period of time, we will come and fix it at no additional cost to you.

If the contractor passes all the above tests, you most likely found one of the few good ones and your bathroom renovation project will be swift and painless.
