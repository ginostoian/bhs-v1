Better Homes Studio has won Best of Houzz Service Award - 2023

We are thrilled to share the best and most flattering news: Better Homes Studio is a Best of Houzz Service 2023 Winner!!

Normally it is not in our nature to brag or bring our achievements to the fore, we prefer to enjoy discreetly the smile of our satisfied customers when we see them delighted with the final result, but when your business has been recognized by the Houzz community of more than 65 million homeowners and home design enthusiasts for the quality of your work or customer service, this is a big deal and this award cannot go unnoticed and unmentioned.

The Best of Houzz Service awards recognize pros providing great service to their customers, and are based on several factors, including a pro's overall rating on Houzz and client reviews submitted in the year prior to the award period.
We are so proud of everything our team has achieved and honoured to be recognized for the hard work we put in on behalf of our clients.

It is the second Best of Houzz award we have won (in a row), and this makes us extremely happy and proud, especially when these recognitions come from one of the largest and most important niche platforms.

Three years ago, when we decided to showcase our services on this platform, it was one of the best decisions our company could make. Houzz is a place where you can find a community of millions of homeowners, home improvement professionals and design enthusiasts around the world, which gained its notoriety through the seriousness and professionalism of high-quality services over time.

If you want to do any type of renovation, from a simple bathroom remodel, a full house renovation to big home makeovers like an extension or a basement conversion, searching a reputable contractor on Houzz is a great place to start.

They offer you recommendations for contractors based on your specific need and location, giving you access to their profiles showcasing past work, reviews and other relevant information. Even if you are completely new to the home improvement scene, their team will guide you through the whole process of finding a great contractor.

This award is an honour even more when we know that in addition to the fact that it is offered by some of the best in the field, this award is won due to the undisputed opinions of the customers.

Our company has been active in this field of services for over 10 years and every time, we have tried to offer the highest quality services, the wishes and needs of our clients being the most important. Professionalism, seriousness, transparency and flexibility to any client's needs are the most important assets that we have built with great care and attention in order to deliver the most satisfactory results for our clients.

We always try to offer complete services and detailed, explicit and transparent quotes, without hidden costs. We are always in direct contact with our clients, they have the opportunity at any time to find out details about their project and to ask any question that worries them. We believe that communication is the key to success when it comes to building a clear relationship between us and our clients, for this reason, the project manager keeps constant contact with them.

From the moment the contract was signed, we constantly offer updates from the field, so that the client is always up to date with the progress of the works.

All these things are reflected in this award, which is strictly based on the undisputed opinion of the customers, and for that we very much thank you.

Thank you for choosing and for trusting us, for letting us be part of the realisation of your long-dreamed-of projects.

It means a lot to us to know that all the work done by our team is appreciated, and this gives us confidence and energy to make things even better and more beautiful in the future.

Every satisfied customer is part of our family, which we want to grow larger year over year and with your help, become the best in London’s construction and renovation scene.

Thank you again for this award, we are honoured and delighted, and this recognition makes us even more motivated to offer the most spectacular results.
