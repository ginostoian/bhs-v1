2023 lighting trends for your bathrooms design

Without a doubt, lighting plays an important role in setting the tone of any space and the right lighting design can make all the difference. From minimalist and sleek designs to luxurious oases, bathrooms are places where we can truly rejuvenate and relax and because of this, few well-placed fixtures can make all the difference in terms of function and style.

When it comes to interior lighting, bathroom lighting is probably given the least consideration of all the rooms in the house, but in recent years we have started to put more and more emphasis on these details. Good lighting in your bathroom helps create a place to recharge and relax and when it's done right, the payoff is great. After all, this is the place where you start and end your day. Starting from this idea, we are taking a look at some of the hottest lighting trends for 2023.

It is not enough just to invest in design, because even the most carefully decorated room can be destroyed by wrongly chosen lighting. At the same time, lighting can be a secret weapon for interior design enthusiasts. The lamps set the atmosphere you need but also offer those small important touches in the decoration of a room.

Ample lights are recommended to be placed where it is needed for showers, shaving, or putting on makeup, while other light sources enhance the overall mood of the room. Applied with precision, bathroom lighting ideas have the capacity to influence a person during different points of the day, to revitalise in the morning and to relax in the evening

The latest bathroom lighting trends will offer inspiration and help you illuminate your bathroom ideas with style.

Backlit Mirrors

One of the easiest ways to bring an element of sophistication and glamour into the decor of the bathroom is by incorporating backlighting behind a mirror frame. Not only does this give the space more visual interest, but it also allows you to adjust the atmosphere to suit your needs and one of the best parts about backlighting is that it ensures even distribution of light around the mirror, unlike wall-mounted lights on the sides or top of the mirror.

Minimalistic Interior Lamps

The use of minimalistic interior lamps on bathroom walls trend seems to continue in 2023 as well. This design remains to everyone's liking because, besides the peaceful and spa-like atmosphere, this style also saves some valuable space. The same effect is given by using LED strip lights, these being often applied to create unique accent lighting and also they are perfect for highlighting certain features in the bathroom, such as a beautiful mirror or a decorative element.

Dramatic Lighting

If you’re looking for something truly unique, unusual shapes, surprising materials and bold colours can all make dramatic or unexpected lighting fixtures, all adding personality to your bathroom. Taking this into consideration, unusual shapes and styles will add interest to simple bathroom colour schemes without losing practicality.

At the same time, this design can be overwhelming to the eye, so be careful not to use too much decor. A few well-chosen pieces will have a much greater impact than a multitude of pieces that will give a tiring and loaded state.

Make A Modern Statement

The lighting design we choose in the house can give us the desired atmosphere every day. As we have noticed, the trend of 2023 is to incorporate elements in the bathroom that we would normally choose for the main room of the house. For example, choosing a sputnik light, such a modern design might typically be placed in a main room, but the trend is absolutely that if you love a light, there is no reason why you cannot place that element wherever you want, even in the bathroom

Make the light the star of the show and do not be afraid to accessorise the bathroom space with bold accents

Wall lights - a soft approach

Bathroom lighting can be more about creating an atmosphere than anything else and because the trends of 2023 place a great emphasis on the state offered during the bath, wall lights are in trend this year.

Wall lights are used in bathrooms to accentuate particular areas, having a decorative purpose because. These lighting elements are usually not the main source of light, their role is to create an atmosphere where everything is about switching off and relaxing.

A wall light offers a softly illuminating glow, creating a calming, tranquil ambience and spa-like environment (read more about the top bathroom design trends in 2023).

Try a Glamour Style

Who said that chandeliers are not made for bathrooms?

Chandeliers in bathrooms are bold style choices that bring function with a huge gloss of flair. Whether you go with a sconce, a pendant or a flush mount, try to incorporate some crystal lighting into your bathroom lighting set-up. From mini chandeliers to show stopping crystal chandeliers, this style trend is the ideal something extra for every home. 'You need two types of illumination: shadow-free task lighting for functional tasks and ambient lighting for when you simply want to lie back and enjoy a soak,' advises Peter Bowles, founder of Davey Lighting.

The crystals will reflect the light from the light bulbs and in this way, they will create a heavenly effect in your bath space.

We recommend that the chandelier be placed in the centre of the bathroom in order to balance the shower, tub and vanity that line the walls. If the room has a freestanding tub, the chandelier can be hung above it, the two components providing the main visual point.

Light the floor with low-level lighting

Have some fun with your bathroom and create dramatic effects by installing floor-level lighting. Positioned underneath a wall-hung cabinet lighting can create a floating effect or can be set up to show off the surface of decorative tiles.

Low-level PIR (Passive Infrared sensor) lighting that comes on automatically as you enter, provides ideal lighting for night-time visits to the bathroom without having to switch on the main lights, plus it isn’t as harsh on the eyes, keeping the atmosphere of sleep.

What Is The Best Lighting For A Bathroom?

There are four main lighting aspects to consider when choosing bathroom lighting, and layering these effectively will provide the very best illumination. The four areas are:

  • Task refers to helpful lighting for functional activities, such as shaving or applying makeup.

  • Ambience refers to lighting that brings harmony and balance to the space, essential in the bathroom where designers often seek to build a state of relaxation and disconnection
  • Feature lighting gives the space personality. Techniques such as positioning or incorporating a light over a bath can say a lot about a person's style. From a spa-like to a fresh and invigorating environment.

Do I Need A Special Light For The Bathroom?

More than any other room in the home, the bathroom represents a space with a higher degree of risk through the prism of the humid environment and safety needs to be a prime concern when installing lighting in a bathroom.

We recommend looking out for the IP Code when you choose the lights. This classifies and rates the degree of protection against water and also for dust. The IP rating you need depends on the area in the bathroom that we want to illuminate. For example, IP65 can be used in any area of a bathroom or wet room, while IP44 is only suitable 60cm from the water source.

Before buying the lighting fixtures for the bathroom, we recommend consulting with electricians to ensure you have up-to-date safety information.
